MANOUCHEHR POURRAJABI

Manouchehr Pourrajabi I.A.

When I was 6 years old, in the summer evenings I gazed at the stars. Before I fell asleep, I asked my sister (who was a few years older than me) what is in the stars? And she said in a low voice: There is in lions, tigers, leopards and many other animals.She wanted to be a little mysterious.

After all these years when I turn my head to heaven I still have the same feeling. even after all this years. The curiosity that I want to know more! And at the same time that makes me aware that I will never know everything.

And this is exactly what I get my strength and passion from, but in the meantime something has never changed. I still feel like a child being seduced by lights!
